Disaster Preparedness Information Hub

This website serves as a comprehensive Disaster Preparedness Information Hub, dedicated to providing clear and reliable guidance on the three most common natural hazards in the Philippines: earthquakes, typhoons, and volcanic eruptions. It offers essential information and step-by-step instructions on the measures to take before, during, and after each disaster.

Our purpose is to promote awareness, strengthen preparedness, and support community resilience by ensuring that accurate and practical safety information is always within reach.
