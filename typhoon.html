TYPHOON

What is a Typhoon?

A typhoon is a powerful tropical cyclone formed over warm ocean waters. It brings strong winds, heavy rainfall, flooding, and sometimes storm surges. In the Philippines, typhoons enter the PAR (Philippine Area of Responsibility) and are monitored by PAGASA for warnings and updates.
